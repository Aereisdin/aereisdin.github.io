Kiel Sprague

My journey in life has been varied and diverse. I have always been a bit of a free spirit. I chalk that up to my early adult years being spent in the military where I was directed and always under guidance. I’ve always felt that life is a journey that is about experiences and interactions with people and places. The joys of life can only be discovered and enjoyed by embracing the unknown and getting outside your comfort zone. My biggest source of confidence has always been my ability to learn, adapt and overcome any challenge. I always move forward and strive to encourage others to as well.

Family

My inspiration has always been my family. I love spending time with my weird and quirky family and seeing them happy. My spouse and dog are my number one inspiration. They keep me grounded and sane through life’s ups and downs. We love to travel and go on adventures together.

Code

Coding, web development and engineering is something that I have really embraced. I have always dabbled and played with HTML, PHP, and some CSS but I was struck one day while listening to a podcast called “Code Newbies”. I could relate to the various places the different people who were being interviewed were coming from. I shared a lot of the same backstory. So I really started looking into code and what it meant to do this thing called development. I was floored and that’s all it took to set me off on this new grand adventure… inspired, creative and addictive. Sure there are problems in code but it’s our job to solve them.
